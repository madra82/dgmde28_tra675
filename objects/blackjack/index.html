Let's Play Blackjack!

Object

Get as close to 21 as possible, without exceeding 21.

Rules

The Deal

  • You and the Dealer will each receive one card face up.
  • You will each receive a second card but the Dealer's card is face down.

Your Play

  • You may "stick", or "twist" as many times as you choose unless you go bust.
  • Picture cards have a value of 10.
  • Aces may be valued as a 1 or 11; the computer will use whichever value gets you closer to 21.

Dealer's Play

  • If the dealer's total is 16 or under, another card will be dealt.
  • Once the dealer's total is at least 17, the dealer will stick.

Betting

  • You may bet any amount in your Wallet.
  • A normal win will pay out twice your bet; a natural Blackjack will pay out three times your bet.
  • At the end of a round, you will be offered the choice to roll over your wallet and play again or restart which will put $500 back in your wallet.
