Background Info

My name is Trudy. I live on the Gulf Coast of Florida but I grew up in Ireland and I also lived in London before moving to the US in 2017. I lived in New York and Chicago before moving to Florida.

I am not currently pursuing any degree or certificate. I have worked as a tax attorney for over 15 years. In 2020, possibly as part of some kind of mid-life crisis (!), I started picking up some courses at Harvard Extension. I started with literature and philosophy courses as I have always been interested in these subjects but, last semester, I decided to try Fundamentals of Website Development. This caught my interest for a couple of reasons:

  • I would like to start a website to track the progress of our home building project; and
  • I have changed course slightly at work and I am now focused on developing tax technology.

Although we are using graph technology at work, we are using a single page web-application to access the graph and much of the background relies on html and JavaScript. While my role is more focused on the tax law underlying the application, I am more and more interested in what is going on behind the scenes. Fundamentals of Website Development has already proven helpful at work and I am excited to learn a bit more.
